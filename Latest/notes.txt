Setup Checklist
Step	Action
1	In Azure DevOps → Library → Variable Groups, create DAST-Secrets.
2	Add variables:
CX_APIKEY (secret) – your Checkmarx One API key
TEAMS_WEBHOOK_URL (secret) – Teams incoming webhook URL
3	Add the above three files to your repository under /dast/.
4	Commit to main branch — pipeline will trigger automatically every month.
5	After run: download results under Pipelines → Artifacts → DAST_Reports and check your Teams channel.
